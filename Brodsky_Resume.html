The Engineering Academy as a Freshman


The Engineering Academy has been one of the best things that have happened to me, it lifted me up when I got the email that I got in during this time of COVID-19. A few factors influenced me before this. The memorable guest speakers, and a few other factors.

The guest speaker that influenced me the most where the ones from Kettering University and the Python Programmer from Chevron. The one from Kettering shined a light to my future after high school. A future that might be a future in Flint, Michigan. The one from Chevron showed me that if you know what you are doing and know the logic behind it, that you can do pretty much anything with programming. These guest speakers really showed me my future and opened my eyes.
